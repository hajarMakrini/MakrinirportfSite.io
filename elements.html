The Beginning..

My journey with ALX Africa began on August 10, 2023. My goal was clear: to become a proficient software engineering student and enhance my computer science skills. At first, the thought of an eight-month program seemed daunting, but as the days passed, tackling task after task, I found myself growing and adapting to the challenges.

how was my journey & challenges:

To be honest, the journey wasn’t easy. With each deadline and new project, self-doubt and stress often crept in, making me question my ability to complete the program. Juggling my studies at another faculty (academic studies) added to the challenge, creating a whirlwind of anxiety and fear of failure. But amidst the struggles, the motivating words from the ALX team — “Do hard things!” — and their unwavering support inspired me to push through
